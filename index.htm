What is OrdacraftUA?

We are a workshop creating unique handmade jewelry inspired by LARP, fantasy, and ethnic designs. Perfect for cosplay enthusiasts and art lovers alike!

Why leave Etsy?

Etsy's fees are outrageous! We want to offer you better prices and a more personal shopping experience without the middleman. Say goodbye to overpriced trinkets!

What types of jewelry?

From mystical pendants to rugged post-apocalyptic pieces, our collection is as diverse as your imagination. Each piece tells a story!

How to contact us?

You can reach us via our contact form on the website or through our social media channels. We love hearing from fellow adventurers!

Do you accept custom orders?

Absolutely! We thrive on creativity. Share your ideas, and let’s craft something magical together!

Where to find us?

Follow us on social media for updates, sneak peeks, and exclusive offers. Join our community of dreamers!
